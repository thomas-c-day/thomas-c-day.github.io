How assembling multicellular bodies is constrained by physical and mechanical challenges

My research is broadly targeted towards one of the ultimate questions of evolutionary history: how did complex, genetically regulated multicellular organisms arise from their unicellular ancestors? A great deal of research has targeted this question, yet there are many pieces of the process that are unsolved or unknown. My work has focused on integrating physical considerations to the fundamentally noisy and difficult process of assembling a multicellular body.
