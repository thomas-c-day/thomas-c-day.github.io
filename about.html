About Me

I am currently a PhD student at the Georgia Institute of Technology since the summer of 2017. My advisor is Prof. Peter Yunker .

But I haven't always lived in Atlanta! I grew up in Haddam, Connecticut and I attended Lafayette College for my undergraduate degree in physics.

While at Lafayette College, I competed in Track & Field and Cross Country and I learned to fall in love with distance running. While my mileage has decreased significantly since I've gone to Georgia Tech, I have still found some time to train for and run the New York City marathon (which was brutal). Most recently, I've joined the club triathlon team, and I competed in my first triathlon in September 2018.

In my (little remaining) free time I like to play guitar. But for now, most of my effort goes into my research.
